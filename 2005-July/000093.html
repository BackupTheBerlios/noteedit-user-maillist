Eric,

I am also using NoteEdit for transcribing classical guitar music. ABC does not 
handle polyphony, but abcm2ps does. NoteEdit's abc output is compatible with 
abcm2ps, i.e. when you use multiple voices in a staff, the resulting abc file 
is polyphonic and needs abcm2ps for printing. This works very well. Don't 
know about fingering, though.

See: http://moinejf.free.fr

Best regards, Leon.
