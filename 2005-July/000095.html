Hi Jean,

to help you we might need more info. Anyway, I what comes to my mind:

1) There's no hint what at all causes the problem, so you could post a
snippet of music (.not or .ly) to the list. We could check if we can
print it. Generally, there are no overall problem, more singular
problems in details.

2) Your lily version seems to be out of date. A stable version, 2.6.1,
would be recommended.

3) If it turns out to be a NoteEdit problem, we would fix it for the
stable versions lilypond 2.4.x and 2.6.

Recently I updated the neccessary packages on my machine for lilypond
and got a working 2.6.1. It seems to be very compatible with NoteEdit
2.8.0, which exports in lilypond 2.4.2 style. A problem arises in
special characters of lyrics and titles. The now partially missing
backend of TeX needs different encondings now for texts, preferably
latin1. But lilypond doesn't chocke on those, instead, makes it as
beatifull as it can :)

If your problem pushes the lilypond 2.6 adaption, that would be only
good ...
