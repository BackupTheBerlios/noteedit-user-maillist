Confirmed. The problem is that it places the volume signs before the
rest. Volume signs before notes are ok. It's definitely NoteEdit's bug.
However, I'd place the volume signs always before notes. It's a common
practice. This way NoteEdit exports fine. If you want to change staff
volume globally, you should do that in Staff properties dialog.
