Hallo ED,

I had a lot of trouble with Suse 10.1 too. (In former times I used Noteedit 
under Suse 9 and everything worked fine.)

To solve my problem I uninstalled Noteedit 2.80 and took the Noteedit 2.81 
tar.gz and compiled it by myself at /usr/local/src . I had to add some libs 
that ./configure asked for (libtse3 was not so easy to be found, so I took 
all tse3-stuff from the Suse-DVD that was offered). Starting the programm is 
not so comfortible as before (prompt in spite of menue) but everything works 
well.
