> 
> The completion of hard-attach decorations and articulation signs is easy to be 
> done in abc.

I called them ``hard-attached'' decorations because one cannot 
edit/add/change the accents/articulations independantly of their carrier 
notes in NoteEdit.

> But the insertion of microtonals in Canorus would be very helpful for a 
> correct playback of ones composition. No more laborious tests with 
> Zynaddsubfx. 

Have not still you tried microabc? You can generate microtonal MIDI 
files with abc2midi, from a ABC file with aliases for the microtonal 
pitches.

Latest version of the program:
http://br.geocities.com/hfmlacerda/abc/microabc-2006-07-29.zip

Margo Schulter has used microabc to typeset her music using the Sagittal 
(http://users.bigpond.net.au/d.keenan/sagittal/Sagittal.pdf) microtonal 
notation system:
http://www.bestii.com/~mschulter/microabc_neomedieval.zip
