There are some music-specific distributions like Agnula Demudi
(Debian/Ubuntu-based), Studio 64 (for 64bit machines) and a
software archive called CCRMA for music apps build for RedHat
Fedora (maybe even a complete CCRMA based distribution exists).
For SuSE you should take a look at jacklab. Also multimedia distributions
exists like dynebolic (Agnula actually aims to be a multimedia distribution
as well).
