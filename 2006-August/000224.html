To Phil:
you can try to make this in Ubuntu:
apt-get source noteedit
cd noteedit-2.8.0

in mainframewidget.ccp you must change line:
keys_ = new KAccel(this);
to
keys_ = new KAccel((QWidget*)this->parent();

then
fakeroot dpkg-buildpackage
cd...
su -c dpkg -i noteedit*.deb

In my situation (Ubuntu 6.06) it works good.
The update manager even says you may change your version for update:not
do it! Then when the new 2.8.1 will in the ubuntu repositories, we will
look at.
