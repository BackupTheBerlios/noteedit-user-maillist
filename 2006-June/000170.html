> changed to its enharmonic equivalent?

I just tried this and it works for me.  I started a new song with the 
key ofEb.  I entered for quarter notes on the bottom line E (treble 
clef).  I then highlighted the last note and selected Tools -> Set all 
to sharp.  It  converted only the selected note to d#.  I hope this helps.
